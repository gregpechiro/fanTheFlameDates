About

Q: Who is Fan the Flame Dates?

A: House on the Rock Family Ministries!

We are a Christian ministry committed to helping the Church start and strengthen her ministries to men, marriages, and families so that they reflect the unconditional love of God to the world.

We believe strong families build strong churches and communities and that strong families are nurtured by a healthy marriages! That is why we have launched these FAN THE FLAME DATES … to help you strengthen your marriage!

We present over 100 Biblically-based family life workshops a year … and have ministered to over 70,000 families … we’d love to serve your church …

Please visit our main website, www.hotrfm.org, for more information.
